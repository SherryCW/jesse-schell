## Quality gate

Before delivering a recommendation, verify:

- It names one decision and one largest risk.
- Every lens can change a decision, artifact, threshold, or observation.
- Facts, interpretations, and unknowns are separated.
- Success and failure are behavioral, not merely attitudinal.
- First-, third-, and thirtieth-session claims are not conflated.
- Commercial success is not used as proof of player value or ethical acceptability.
- The recommendation includes rollback, stop, or remediation conditions where harm is plausible.
